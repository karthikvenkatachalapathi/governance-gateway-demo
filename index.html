What to enter

  1. Issuer URL: the base OIDC issuer from your identity provider, usually the value shown as “Issuer” or “OpenID Provider URL”. Do not include /.well-known/openid-configuration.
  2. Client ID: the OAuth/OIDC client identifier from the identity-provider app you created for this control plane.
  3. Client secret: the client secret from that app. Leave blank on later saves to keep the existing saved secret.
  4. Redirect URI: copy this exact callback into the identity provider’s allowed redirect/callback URLs: /api/oidc/callback on this control-plane host.
  5. Allowed email domains: optional comma-separated domains, for example example.com, company.com. Leave blank to allow any domain that your identity provider authenticates.

Turn this on only after the issuer URL, client ID, client secret, and redirect URI are filled in and saved.
